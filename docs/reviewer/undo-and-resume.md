# Undo and resume

## Undo

**Undo last** reverses your most recent decision and returns that paper to the queue.

## Resume

Decisions auto-save after each card. Close the browser and sign in again — you continue where you left off.

Skipped papers return to the queue after all undecided papers are screened.

## Session

Reviewer password is not saved in the browser. You must sign in again after closing all tabs (JWT may still be in session until expiry).
