# Summary and exclusion buckets

## During screening

- **← / Arrow left** or a short swipe left → **Exclude** (no prompt)
- **Hold far left** while dragging → vertical **buckets** appear; release on a bucket to exclude with that reason
- **↓ Maybe** and **→ Include** work as before — no bucket prompt mid-screen

## After screening

When all papers are decided, you enter the **Assign reasons** step for every Exclude and Maybe paper:

- Buckets are listed vertically on the **left**
- Tap one or more buckets per paper, then **Next**
- **Skip all** to finish without tagging

## Summary view

Open **Summary** from the header anytime to review decisions and bucket tags.

## Admin configuration

Review admin → **Exclusion buckets** — add/remove labels with **Add**, remove with **×**, or **Reset defaults**.

Buckets are stored encrypted per reviewer (blinded until CSV export).
